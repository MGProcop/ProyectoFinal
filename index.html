Lodge Overview - La Estrella Wetland

This place is a provincial nature reserve of 400.000 hectares and has been declared one of the 7 wonders of Argentina. It is the second largest wetland in the country and is home to a rich diversity of fauna. Visitors can encounter species such as the pecarí de collar, oso hormiguero, tapir, yaguareté, aguará guazú, ciervo de los pantanos, yacaré, carpincho, oso melero, alligator, and capybara. Designated as an AICA area, more than 300 species of birds find their habitat here, including the majestic jabirú. Stay with us and live the experience!

Sustainability in action

The Jabirú Trust is a community and wildlife conservation trust supported by ecotourism, also focusing on education and social responsibility initiatives. Our conservation model is built on meaningful community partnerships, biodiversity preservation and wildlife management. We aim to implement a workable formula between conservation and social projects.
